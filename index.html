About Us

The IET SRM Student Chapter is a body that acts as a bridge between IET India and the IET student members of SRM University as well as conduct various even. We are working under the IET Motto, working to engineer a better world. We conduct events in-campus and off-campus which we aim at for delivering knowledge to all sections os the society and making places around us better.

The IET is one of the world’s largest engineering institutions with over 160,000 members in 127 countries. It is also the most multidisciplinary – to reflect the increasingly diverse nature of engineering in the 21st century. The IET is working to engineer a better world by inspiring, informing and influencing our members, engineers and technicians, and all those who are touched by, or touch, the work of engineers.
